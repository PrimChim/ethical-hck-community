Wifi Hacking Event

July 24, 2022

Ethical HCK community organized a Wifi Hacking event that took place on July 24th, 2022. The event was held at the BIT faculty and was a huge success, with over 70 enthusiastic students in attendance. The event was aimed at educating students about Wifi and how to hack it ethically. The event featured a range of interactive sessions led by experienced community members, which covered various topics related to Wifi hacking, including Wifi penetration testing, vulnerability scanning, and network monitoring.

The attendees had the opportunity to witness a live demonstration of Wifi hacking techniques, which showcased the latest hacking tools and methods used by ethical hackers to secure Wifi networks. This practical demonstration allowed students to learn about the process of hacking a Wifi network and the steps needed to protect against such attacks.

Being one of the first ever events to be hosted by the Ethical HCK, the session went well overall and this could be called a successful work from the Ethical HCK team.

“Saurav Adhikari”

Overall, the Wifi Hacking event organized by Ethical HCK was a great success, providing students with valuable knowledge and practical skills related to Wifi hacking. The event helped to raise awareness about the importance of ethical hacking practices and the need for individuals to safeguard their Wifi networks against potential attacks.
